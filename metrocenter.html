**Metrocenter** is a marketplace and outdoor shopping complex in the northern part of [Neon Valley]. Metrocenter is mostly notable due to its entrances called *gates*, all of which are connected to the city's major public transport hubs: * Metro line 9A at Northern and Northwestern gates * Metro line 3A at Northern and Eastern gates * Tram line 55G at Southern, Southeastern and Southwestern gates * Railway 189 at Eastern and Northeastern gates The Metrocenter Western Gate is open to Metrocenter Plaza.
