**Rattlesnake Brewery** is a brewing company in [Neon Valley], distributor of the famous beer brands [From Above] and [Rattlesnake]. Founded in V422, it's the oldest and one of the most notable alcohol manufacturing companies in the city. **Rattlesnake Brewevery Headquarters** is an enormous brewery, bottling plant and office complex in the southern part of the Perimeter. Notable locations near to Meteor RBHQ: * *Beer City*, subway station for Metro line 3A as well as a transit point for Railway 77. * *Beer City Offices*, transit point for Railway 189 and Tram Line 19G.
