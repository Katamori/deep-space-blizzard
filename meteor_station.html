**Meteor Station** is an [NVG](neon_valley_government)-owned military and logistical complex and restricted area in the northwestern part of [Neon Valley]. Formerly, it was the crash site of the [alien Mothership](alien_technology~mothership) named Meteor. A huge pedestrian buffer zone around is left free for guards and related services. The entirety of the facility and its buffer zone is off-limits for every civilian, and even most military personnel. Notable locations near to Meteor Station: * *Watchtower*, terminus of Metro line 9A. * **[Perimeter Gate] Sierra**, one of the 26 outer gates providing entrance to the inner districts.
