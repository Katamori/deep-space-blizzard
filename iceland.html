**Iceland** is a volcanic island the North Atlantic Ocean and home of a pre-[Blizzard] parlamentary republic. It is notable for becoming a major alien military operation center [during the invasion of Earth](timelime~1989-2064), which has gradually become more important up until its final destruction. As of 269RDA, Iceland is barren and uninhabitable due to 85% of its surface being covered in leftover [Frost].
