**Blank Empire** is the anecdotal name of the uninhabitable region in the immediate surroundings of an [Alien Mothership](mothership) crash site between [the Zone] and [Sanctuary]. While the impact itself originally occured [in a mountainous region](Nordline), its sheer power eroded the nearby rocks (most likely as a result of a [Frost] chain reaction), flattening a very wide area around it. The Blank Empire is unanimously considered the most hostile region of the [Ancient Nation land](the_ancient_nation)
