A **junta** is an authoritarian government state led by high ranking officers of a serving military. In [Sanctuary], **the Junta** refers to the last surviving period of [the Ancient Nation]. After decades of decay, the societal collapse occured in various parts of the Ancient Nation which led to a violent collapse of the reign of the time, leading to a military dictatorship. Endless wars and the use of mass destruction weapons destroyed it and the rest of the nation before [the end of the New Age.](timeline~starshift_period)
